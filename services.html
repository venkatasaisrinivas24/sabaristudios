Post-Processing Excellence

Motion Capture Services

Sabari Studios delivers a full range of advanced post-processing solutions for motion capture data used in animation, visual effects, films, television, commercials, and gaming industries.

Our dedicated team of skilled artists and technicians ensures high-quality, production-ready mocap data that seamlessly integrates into your pipeline. With a secure client portal and streamlined workflows, we guarantee fast turnaround times without compromising on quality.

Our Post-Processing Services Include:

Data Cleanup & Tracking

Precise cleaning and refining of raw motion capture data for maximum accuracy.

Re-targeting

Mapping motion capture data to diverse character rigs, from humans to creatures.

Facial Tracking & Re-targeting

Capturing and transferring lifelike facial expressions with natural detail.

In-Game Cutscenes & Locomotion

Smooth animations for games, including loops, transitions, and pose-to-pose actions.

Cinematic Animations

High-end tailored animations for films, OTT, television, and commercial projects.
